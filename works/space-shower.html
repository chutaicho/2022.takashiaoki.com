The series of short films for a Japanese music television channel Space Shower TV in collaboration with Daisuke Fujiwara and Gerard Rallo. The brief is to revitalize their motion identities in 2021. We created five films to make fresh and modern images to the viewers. A film per month is released from March 2021.
